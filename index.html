Level-Up Learning

Joseph Kawiecki, John Ramthun
October 2023 - December 2023

pdf / code / video

In this project, we investigate policy transfer learning for video game platforming (Super Mario). We review various transfer learning methods, apply them to the Proximal Policy Optimization (PPO) algorithm, and conduct experiments. Our results show that while beneficial for other algorithms, current approaches to transfer learning do not work well for PPO in video gameplay.

Survey on Task Transfer Learning for Robotics

Joseph Kawiecki
October 2023 - December 2023

pdf / code / video

RL for robot motion training has shown great promise, often performing on par or better than traditional motion controllers. But still, many solutions require specific knowledge of the robot, such as kinematic makeup, for use. This project will delve into robot motion policy transfer more deeply, explore a few recent methods, and conduct experiments with the D4RL dataset.
